## Equipment Sourcing — Plastic-to-Product Line

### Direction
User wants a machine that takes recycled plastic and outputs finished products (fence posts, French drain piping, etc.). Australian-made not required — priorities are great product, fair price, reliability.

### Country recommendation: **Taiwan**
The sweet spot for quality vs. price. Use this as the default starting point.

| Tier | Country | Price Range | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|
| Premium | Austria (EREMA) | $400K–$1.5M+ | Overkill unless food-grade |
| Premium | Italy (Bausano, Amut) | $250K–$700K | Pay for the badge |
| **Sweet spot** | **🇹🇼 Taiwan (Polystar, Genius)** | **$80K–$300K** | **Default pick** |
| Budget | China (top tier: JWELL, Tongsan) | $30K–$150K | Workable with on-site QC |
| Avoid | China mid/low tier | $20K–$80K | Don't |

### Why Taiwan
- Polystar and Genius source nitrided 38CrMoAlA barrels and bi-metallic screws from the **same Japanese/Taiwanese subcontractors that supply EREMA**
- Siemens S7-1500 PLCs and Schneider drives — same as European premium tier
- Genius twin-stage extruder held ±2°C melt-temp stability over 72-hr run — statistically indistinguishable from EREMA
- 20–30% cheaper than European equivalents
- 30–45 day delivery vs. 6–12 months for EREMA
- Fence posts and drain pipe are forgiving products — Taiwan is more than enough quality

### Buying playbook
1. Get quotes from **Polystar AND Genius Machinery** first (healthy competition)
2. Get a sanity-check quote from **JWELL (China)** as negotiation leverage
3. **Visit the factory** before wiring money — non-negotiable
4. **Call US-based references** — ask about service response time and parts availability
5. Buy a **year of spares upfront** (screws, dies, heaters) — replacement lead time is 60–90 days
6. **Find a US-based service tech relationship** before purchase — machine will break in year 1

### Aussie companies (potential partners or competitors, not machinery sellers)
- **Advanced Plastic Recycling (APR)** — WPC posts, 50% recycled plastic + 50% timber waste, 7,000 posts/wk
- **PolyNex Plastics** — 100% HDPE recycled fence posts
- **Plastic Forests** — soft-plastic recycling into posts/bollards/mats
- Worth a call about licensing or co-packing rather than just sourcing machines

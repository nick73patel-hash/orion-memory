# Project 10 — AI Exteriors Company

## What this is
An exterior home improvement company (roofing, siding, windows, painting, etc.) that uses AI to eliminate traditional sales, canvassing, and marketing costs. The model: a homeowner submits photos of their home online, the AI analyzes them and generates an instant quote — no sales rep visit required. This allows higher subcontractor pay, minimal project manager overhead, and lean operations.

## The core innovation
Traditional exteriors companies spend heavily on door-to-door canvassing, sales commissions, and marketing. This model replaces all of that with:
1. **Photo submission** → homeowner uploads exterior photos via website
2. **AI analysis** → measures surfaces, identifies materials, detects damage
3. **Instant online quote** → professional, accurate, no human needed for initial estimate
4. **Streamlined close** → homeowner accepts digitally, job is scheduled
5. **Subcontractors get paid more** — savings from eliminated sales/marketing go to the crew

## Siding P&L Model (from May 2026 research + user real-world numbers)

### Industry benchmarks (standard siding company)
- Typical job size: $15K–$60K+ depending on home size
- Industry gross margin: 30–45% (materials + sub labor eat 55–70%)
- Standard sub labor for 20 sq siding job: ~$11K
- Standard materials for 20 sq job: ~$12K
- Sales/canvassing/marketing: 8–15% of revenue (eliminated in this model)
- Net profit industry average: 5–15% after all overhead

### Our model — per job (20 sq siding job)
| Line Item | Amount | % of Revenue |
|---|---|---|
| Market rate (what competitors charge) | $62,000 | — |
| **AI Exteriors price** | **$50,000** | 100% |
| Materials | ($12,000) | 24% |
| Sub labor (premium — $3K above market) | ($14,000) | 28% |
| **Gross profit** | **$24,000** | **48%** |
| Direct admin / job mgmt | (~$1,150) | ~2.3% |
| Back-end overhead allocation | (~$1,314) | ~2.6% |
| **Net profit per job** | **~$19,936** | **~40%** |

### Back-end fixed overhead (annual, ~$196,100/yr)
- Owner salary, insurance, software/AI tools, marketing, admin/bookkeeping, phone/vehicle, legal/accounting, office/misc
- At 52 jobs/yr: ~$3,773/job fully loaded
- Reported as $2,464/job (4.9% of revenue) in document — combining direct admin + back-end overhead

### Year 1–3 projections (from business plan)
| Year | Jobs | Revenue | Net Profit | Margin |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Year 1 | 52 (1/wk) | $2.6M | ~$909K | ~35% |

### Key competitive advantages in this model
1. Price 19% below market ($50K vs $62K) — easier close, more referrals
2. Subs paid $3K above market — better crews, less turnover
3. Zero door-to-door sales / commissions — all savings go to margin + crew
4. AI photo quoting = no estimator labor for initial quote
5. Lean back office = more jobs per admin dollar

# Project 9 — Investor & Entrepreneur Network App

## What this is
A platform that connects three types of people:
1. **Like-minded investors** looking for opportunities
2. **Successful people with businesses** looking for capital or partnerships
3. **Ambitious young people** who want to work with entrepreneurs on startups

Think of it as a curated, high-trust matchmaking network — LinkedIn meets AngelList meets a mentorship community.

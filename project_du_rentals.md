# Project 12 — Student Rentals (University of Denver)

## What this is
Acquiring fixer-upper homes near the University of Denver campus, renovating them, and renting rooms to DU students. Target: high-demand, walkable-to-campus housing in a proven market with strong, reliable student tenant base.

## Status
🟡 Starting — concept defined, no work started yet

## Partners
- Solo (user-led)

## Why DU is a strong market
- Private university — students (and parents) have higher income and willingness to pay
- Strong academic reputation = consistent enrollment, no enrollment cliff risk
- Walkable neighborhoods near campus (University Park, University Hills) are desirable
- Fixer-uppers = forced appreciation + BRRRR strategy potential
- Denver rental market = strong long-term appreciation

## Target strategy
- **Buy fixer-uppers** in University Park / University Hills / Wellshire area
- **Renovate** to student-friendly standard (durable, functional, clean — not luxury)
- **Rent by the room** — 4 or 5 bed houses maximize income per property
- **Individual leases per student** — reduces vacancy risk (one student leaving doesn't kill the lease)
- **Furnished** — students from out of state need furnished housing, allows premium pricing

# Project 12 — College Student Rentals (DU & Cheyenne)

## What this is
Acquiring or leasing rental properties near college campuses and renting them to college students. Two target markets:

1. **Denver, CO — University of Denver (DU)** area
   - Buy fixer-uppers, renovate, rent to students
   - Or rent existing homes and sublease to students (rental arbitrage)

2. **Cheyenne, WY — Laramie County Community College (LCCC)**
   - Find rental homes near campus and rent to college students
   - Lower price point market, potentially strong yield

## Status
🟡 Starting — concept defined, no work started yet

## Partners

## Why this is smart
- College students = reliable, recurring demand every academic year
- DU is a private university with higher-income students (parents often pay)
- Cheyenne/LCCC is a lower cost market with potentially strong cap rates
- Fixer-uppers at DU = forced appreciation + BRRRR strategy potential
- Existing experience managing properties (Condo Assistant) gives an operational edge

- 8 days / 7 nights
- Denver pickup and drop-off at DIA
- ~1,000 mile round trip (Denver → Glenwood Springs → Palisade → Moab → Vail → Denver)

## 8-Day Itinerary

**Day 1 — Denver → Glenwood Springs · Hot Springs + Mountain Bike**
- Denver pickup at DIA, welcome drinks, tour overview en route
- Drive I-70 west through the Rockies (~160 miles)
- Glenwood Canyon overlook stop
- Arrive Glenwood Springs · Check into RV resort
- 1-hour guided e-mountain bike loop — easy scenic warm-up ride
- Evening: Glenwood Hot Springs · group dinner in town

**Day 2 — Glenwood Springs → Palisade → Sports Car Drive → Moab**
- Morning drive to Palisade, CO (~55 miles)
- Swap into 4 sports cars garaged in Palisade
- Epic 4-hour drive: Palisade → Montrose → Telluride and back through the San Juan Mountains (~150 miles)
- Return to Palisade, re-board RV
- Drive to Moab (~90 miles) · arrive late afternoon
- Set up base camp at Moab Valley RV Resort
- Camp dinner, guide briefs the week

**Day 3 — Moab · Defender Overland + Waterfall Hike**
- Morning: 3-hour private Defender overland tour — off-road backcountry, canyon rim views
- Afternoon: hike to waterfall + alpine lake in the La Sal Mountains (45 min from Moab)
- Sunset at camp · group dinner

**Day 4 — Moab · Colorado River Rafting + Easy Bike Ride**
- Morning: full Colorado River rafting run, Class II–III rapids · riverside lunch
- Afternoon: easy e-bike cruise (Moab Brand Trails or Mill Creek Canyon)
- Evening campfire

**Day 5 — Moab · Side-by-Sides · Hell's Revenge Self-Drive**
- Full morning: Hell's Revenge slickrock UTV trail — guests self-drive side-by-sides
- Afternoon: rest at camp, swim, optional spa
- Sunset drinks · dinner in Moab (Desert Bistro or Star Hall)

**Day 6 — Moab · Epic Mountain Bike + Scenic Flight or Hot Air Balloon**
- Morning: epic guided e-mountain bike ride — Slickrock Trail or Whole Enchilada descent
- Afternoon: 45–60 min scenic flight over Canyonlands OR hot air balloon over the canyon at sunset
- Final Moab campfire · guide farewell dinner

**Day 7 — Moab → Vail · Guest Choice Recovery Day**
- Morning pack-up · drive I-70 east back through Colorado (~4.5 hrs)
- Arrive Vail midday — guests are wiped out, they pick what sounds good
- **Guest-choice activity menu (pre-book whichever they want):**
  - 🎣 Fly fishing on the Eagle River — guided half-day
  - 🚠 Gondola ride up Vail Mountain + alpine hike or lunch at the top
  - 🛶 Whitewater kayak or raft on the Eagle River
  - 🚵 Vail mountain biking — groomed trails, all levels
  - 🧖 Spa day — full recovery, massage, hot tub (Vail has world-class options) · **extra cost, guest pays directly**
  - 🍷 Wine tasting + lunch in Vail Village (do nothing, eat well, walk around)
  - 🎿 Summer tubing or adventure ridge activities
- Overnight Vail area RV park · group dinner in Vail Village

**Day 8 — Vail → DIA · Drop-Off**
- Morning drive over the Rockies (~100 miles)
- Optional stop: Idaho Springs for breakfast
- Drop-off at Denver International Airport by midday
- Guide gratuity collected, farewell

## Growth Roadmap

### Vision
5 RV setups per route × 4 routes = **20 total setups** at full scale
Each setup: 20 trips/year → **400 trips/year total**

### Scaling Phases

| Phase | Setups | Routes | Total Trips/yr | Est. Gross | Est. Net* |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Phase 1 — Launch | 1 | 1 (CO/UT) | 20 | ~$540K | ~$206K–$226K |
| Phase 2 — Prove the model | 5 | 1 (CO/UT) | 100 | ~$2.7M | ~$1.0M–$1.1M |
| Phase 3 — Add a route | 10 | 2 routes | 200 | ~$5.4M | ~$2.0M–$2.2M |
| Phase 4 — Full scale | 20 | 4 routes | 400 | ~$10.4M | ~$4.0M–$4.4M |

*Net estimate = setups × per-setup net. Corporate overhead (ops managers, booking staff, marketing) grows at scale — budget 10–20% of gross for this at Phase 3+.

### What Scales Per Setup
Each new setup needs its own RV, Defender, trailer, and bike set. Per-setup economics are roughly the same as Phase 1. Incremental fixed costs per setup: ~$74K/yr (loans + insurance + workers comp).

### What Can Be Shared

- At 5 setups on Product A, evaluate whether 4 cars is enough or if fleet needs to grow
- Booking platform, CRM, and website are fixed cost — scales free
- General liability policy may have volume discount at scale

## 8-Day Itinerary

**Day 1 — Denver → Glenwood Springs**
- Denver pickup, welcome drinks, tour overview
- Drive I-70 west through the Rockies
- Glenwood Canyon overlook stop
- Overnight Glenwood Springs RV resort (~160 miles)
- Evening: Glenwood Hot Springs, group dinner

**Day 2 — Glenwood Springs → Moab**
- Drive through Grand Junction into Utah (~185 miles)
- Stop: Colorado National Monument
- Arrive Moab, set up base camp at Moab Valley RV Resort
- Orientation e-bike ride on easy Moab trail
- Sunset at Dead Horse Point State Park
- Camp dinner, guide briefs the week

**Day 3 — Arches National Park + E-Bikes**
- Guided e-bike ride through Arches National Park
- Delicate Arch hike
- Afternoon free time at camp
- Group dinner in Moab

**Day 4 — Colorado River Rafting**
- Full-day whitewater rafting, Class II–III rapids
- Riverside lunch
- Afternoon rest at camp
- Evening campfire

**Day 5 — Side-by-Sides + Canyon Flight**
- Morning: Hell's Revenge slickrock UTV trail
- Afternoon: 45–60 min scenic canyon flight over Canyonlands
- Sunset drinks at camp

**Day 6 — Canyonlands + Free Choice**
- Morning: e-bike ride (White Rim or Corona Arch)
- Afternoon: guest choice (UTV, kayak, spa, Fisher Towers)
- Final group dinner at top Moab restaurant (Desert Bistro / Star Hall)
- Campfire

**Day 7 — Moab → Glenwood Springs**
- Pack up, drive back through Castle Valley (~185 miles)
- Optional stop: Sego Canyon petroglyphs
- Overnight Glenwood Springs
- Hot springs evening

**Day 8 — Glenwood Springs → Denver**
- Morning drive over the Rockies (~160 miles)
- Optional stop: Vail or Idaho Springs for lunch
- Denver drop-off by early afternoon
- Guide gratuity collected, farewell

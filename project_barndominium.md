## Eagle County vs Grand County key differences
- Eagle County labor ~30% over national (vs ~20% Grand County) — ~15% higher on labor items
- Eagle County adds EEOP fee, Transportation Impact Fee, Wildfire Mitigation Fee (~$12K extra)
- Eagle County OWTS regs (eff. March 2026) — PE engineer + licensed EC contractor required (~$10K more on septic)
- Colorado Wildfire Resiliency Code (CWRC, eff. July 2025) — ember-resistant vents required in WUI zone
- Snow load: ~40 PSF at Wolcott (6,990 ft) vs 45 PSF Grand County (~9,000 ft)
- Design temp: -5F to -10F Wolcott vs -20F Grand County

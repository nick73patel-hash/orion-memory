# Perpetual Blue — Southern Grenadines Itinerary

**Vessel:** 2013 Fountaine Pajot Sanya 57 — "Perpetual Blue"
**Captain:** Sean Powell
**Guests:** 6
**Total aboard:** 7 (6 guests + Captain Sean Powell)
**Crew:** Captain only — no chef, no first mate
**Dates:** August 12–21, 2026 · 10 days · 9 nights
**Provisioning:** Self-catering aboard + dining ashore at stops

---

## Route Overview

```
Grand Anse Bay (2 nights: arrival + full beach day)
→ Grand Anse + BBC Beach + Black Bay stop → Bathway / Levera turtles
→ Tyrrel Bay, Carriacou (1 night)
→ Sandy Island + Sculptures → straight to Salt Whistle Bay, Mayreau
→ Tobago Cays (2 nights) → clear SVG → Tyrrel Bay
→ sail south down Grenada's west coast → Grand Anse Bay → ✈️
```

## Day-by-Day

---

### 📅 Day 1 — Tuesday Aug 12 · Arrival · Grand Anse Dinghy Pickup

**2:30pm** — Land at Maurice Bishop International Airport
**~2:45pm** — Taxi from airport to Grand Anse Beach *(~15 min, ~$15 USD)*
**~3:15pm** — Captain Sean meets guests at the waterline with the dinghy — step off the sand directly aboard
*(No marina, no dock — straight from the beach onto the boat. Best way to start a charter.)*

**🏖️ Grand Anse Beach — Evening**
- 2 miles of white sand, calm, crystal clear · swim and settle in
- Sundowners in the cockpit watching the sunset over Grand Anse
- Dinner ashore — La Boulangerie or BB's Crabback in St. George's

**Night 1: Grand Anse Bay**

---

### 📅 Day 2 — Wednesday Aug 13 · Full Day at Grand Anse · Rest + Provisions

*Settle in, explore the beach, stock up for the trip north*

**🏖️ BBC Beach (Morne Rouge Bay) — Morning**
- **Dinghy: ~1nm → 5 min** around the headland
- Sheltered crescent bay, crystal clear turquoise water, much quieter than Grand Anse
- Morning swim, snorkel, totally relaxed · 1.5–2 hrs

**🏖️ Grand Anse Beach — Midday & Afternoon**
- Long walk up the beach · paddleboard · full beach afternoon
- Optional: taxi into St. George's for the spice market, local rum, or lunch in town

**Late Afternoon** — Back aboard · provision run from Budget Marine / IGA for the trip north
- Stock the galley well — breakfast + lunch self-catered for the days ahead

**Sundowners** — cockpit over Grand Anse · last night at the starting point

**Dinner** — La Boulangerie on the beach, or BB's Crabback in St. George's

**Night 2: Grand Anse Bay**

---

### 📅 Day 3 — Thursday Aug 14 · Grand Anse + BBC Beach + Black Bay Stop · Sail to Bathway

*Long but rewarding day — two beaches before departure, one stop underway, arrive Bathway by late afternoon*

**7:00am** — Early start, coffee aboard

**🏖️ Grand Anse Beach — Early Morning**
- Quick swim off the white sand before the day heats up · 45 min

**🏖️ BBC Beach (Morne Rouge Bay) — Morning**
- **Dinghy: ~1nm → 5 min** around the headland · sheltered, crystal clear · 1.5 hrs

**~9:30am** — Back aboard, weigh anchor, sail north up the leeward west coast

**🏖️ Black Bay Beach — Midday Stop** *(beach stop, not overnight)*
- **Sail: ~15nm → ~3 hrs → arrive ~12:30pm**
- *(Just south of Grand Roy Medical Center, west coast Grenada)*
- Dinghy → **3 min** · dark volcanic sand, dramatic, very few visitors · swim + lunch · 1 hr

**~1:30pm** — Continue north, round Grenada's north tip to the northeast coast

**🏖️ Bathway Beach — Late Afternoon Arrival**
- **Sail: ~14nm around the north tip → ~3 hrs → arrive ~4:30pm**
- Protected behind Bathway Reef — calm even when the Atlantic side is rough
- **Dinghy: ~0.5nm → 3 min** · swim, snorkel the reef edge, sundowners on the boat

**🐢 Levera Beach option** *(dinghy 1nm → 5 min)*
- August = peak sea turtle nesting season · Captain Sean assesses swell on the day

**Night 3: Bathway / Levera anchorage, NE Grenada**

---

### 📅 Day 4 — Friday Aug 15 · Bathway → Tyrrel Bay, Carriacou

**8:00am** — Depart, sail north to Carriacou
- **Sail: ~15nm → 3 hrs → arrive Tyrrel Bay ~11:00am**
- Reach/broad reach in the August trades · dolphins common on this crossing

**Lunch aboard, swim, settle in**

**🏖️ Paradise Beach — Afternoon**
*(L'Esterre Bay, west coast of Carriacou)*
- **Dinghy from Tyrrel Bay: ~3nm → 15 min**
- Long calm beach, clear shallow water, zero development · local fishing boats, coconut palms

**Evening** — Walk into Hillsborough · watch the wooden boat builders on the beach
*(Still hand-built using 18th century methods — remarkable)*
**Dinner** — Bogles Round House *(book ahead)* or Lazy Turtle

**Night 4: Tyrrel Bay, Carriacou** *(1 night only — heading north tomorrow)*

---

### 📅 Day 5 — Saturday Aug 16 · Sandy Island + Sculptures → Salt Whistle Bay, Mayreau

*Morning at the two best stops on Carriacou, then sail straight north to Mayreau*

**7:30am** — Depart Tyrrel Bay, head northwest toward Sandy Island

**🏖️ Sandy Island — Morning**
- **Sail: ~3nm → 35 min → arrive ~8:05am**
- White sand spit, coconut palms, zero development — **top 5 beaches in the Caribbean**
- Anchor right off the sand · swim, snorkel · 1.5 hrs
- Local fishermen come by — buy fresh lobster for tonight

**🌊 Underwater Sculpture Park — Mid-Morning**
- **Short reposition from Sandy Island** *(confirm exact site with Captain Sean)*
- Snorkel the coral-encrusted underwater sculptures — incredible marine life · 1.5 hrs

**~11:30am** — Depart, sail north toward Union Island

- **Sail: ~8nm → ~1.5 hrs → arrive Union Island ~1:00pm**
- Clear **into SVG at Clifton Harbour, Union Island** *(allow 30–45 min)*

**~1:45pm** — Depart Union Island → Mayreau
- **Sail: ~5nm → ~1 hr → arrive Salt Whistle Bay ~2:45pm**

**🏖️ Salt Whistle Bay — Afternoon**
- Two perfect white sand crescents separated by a strip of coconut palms
- August: very quiet — may nearly have it to yourselves
- Swim, snorkel, paddleboard, total stillness
- **Late afternoon: hike to Mayreau Village** — 20-min climb → hilltop church → **360° Grenadines panorama**

**Dinner** — Salt Whistle Bay resort beachside, or lobster aboard

**Night 5: Salt Whistle Bay, Mayreau**

---

### 📅 Day 6 — Sunday Aug 17 · Mayreau → Tobago Cays

**9:00am** — Depart Salt Whistle Bay
- **Sail: ~5nm north → ~1 hr → arrive Tobago Cays ~10:00am**
- Anchor inside **Horseshoe Reef** *(10–20ft, flat water, reef-protected)*
- Local fishermen arrive early — buy live lobster right off their boat

**🏖️ Baradal Beach — Mid-Morning**
- **Dinghy: ~2nm → 10–12 min**
- Sea turtles feeding on seagrass at the waterline — completely unafraid
- Snorkel: 60–80ft visibility, hard corals, rays · 1.5–2 hrs

**Afternoon** — Horseshoe Reef south side · **dinghy ~1nm → 5 min** · bigger fish, nurse sharks in the channels

**Sunset** — Cockpit, cocktails, 360° Grenadines panorama

**Night 6: Tobago Cays** ✅ *(Night 1 of 2)*

---

### 📅 Day 7 — Monday Aug 18 · Tobago Cays Day 2 — Full Dinghy Day

**~7:00am** — Local fishermen motor up · buy live lobster off the boat

**🏖️ Petit Tabac — Morning**
- **Dinghy: ~2.5nm → 12–15 min**
- Tiny uninhabited island · *Pirates of the Caribbean* filming location · private, untouched

**Midday — Jamesby & Petit Bateau**
- **Dinghy: ~1–1.5nm → 5–8 min each**
- Explore the remaining cays · flat calm water · turtles all afternoon

**Afternoon** — Back to Baradal for a final snorkel, or float in the flat water of the Horseshoe

**Sunset** — Full Grenadines panorama from the cockpit · cocktails

**Night 7: Tobago Cays** ✅ *(Night 2 of 2)*

---

### 📅 Day 8 — Tuesday Aug 19 · Tobago Cays → Tyrrel Bay, Carriacou

**8:00am** — Depart Tobago Cays, head south toward Union Island

- **Sail: Tobago Cays → Clifton Harbour, Union Island · ~8nm → ~1.5 hrs → arrive ~9:30am**
- Clear **out of SVG at Union Island** *(allow 30 min)*

**~10:00am** — Depart Union Island, sail south to Carriacou
- **Sail: Union Island → Tyrrel Bay · ~15nm → 3 hrs → arrive ~1:00pm**
- *(Clear back into Grenada at Hillsborough — Captain Sean to handle)*

**~1:00pm** — Arrive Tyrrel Bay · lunch, swim, relax afternoon
- One last sundowner at the finest anchorage in the southern Caribbean

**Dinner** — Lazy Turtle or cook aboard

**Night 8: Tyrrel Bay, Carriacou**

---

### 📅 Day 9 — Wednesday Aug 20 · Tyrrel Bay → Grand Anse Bay · Down the West Coast

**7:00am** — Depart Tyrrel Bay, sail south down the leeward (west) side of Grenada

- **Sail: Tyrrel Bay → Grand Anse Bay · ~30nm → ~6 hrs → arrive ~1:00pm**
- Beautiful broad reach/run south · Grenada's volcanic peaks rising ahead
- Scenic west coast passage — Dragon Bay, Grand Roy, St. George's harbour entrance all slide by
- Round Point Salines, drop anchor in Grand Anse Bay — back where it all started

**🏖️ Grand Anse Beach — Afternoon**
- Final swim, paddleboard, soak in the last hours
- Pack bags aboard · sundowners in the cockpit

**Dinner** — BB's Crabback, St. George's *(taxi ~15 min — best restaurant on the island)*

**Night 9: Grand Anse Bay**

---

### 📅 Day 10 — Thursday Aug 21 · Departure

**Morning** — Slow start · final coffee over Grand Anse · one last swim if the mood strikes
**~2:00pm** — Taxi to Maurice Bishop Airport *(~15 min, ~$15)*
**3:30pm** — ✈️ Depart

- **Black Bay Beach** — morning stop only; just south of Grand Roy Medical Center, west coast Grenada
- **Bathway Beach** — protected behind Bathway Reef; calm even when the Atlantic is rough
- **Levera Beach** — peak turtle nesting August; swell permitting — Captain Sean's call on the day
- **Sandy Island** — 35 min from Tyrrel Bay; top 5 Caribbean beaches; buy lobster from fishermen
- **Underwater Sculpture Park** — near Sandy Island; confirm exact coordinates with Captain Sean
- **Salt Whistle Bay** — arrive ~3pm; hike to Mayreau Village for 360° Grenadines panorama
- **Tobago Cays** — 2 nights; reserve mooring if possible; fills up even in August
- **Day 9 west coast run** — scenic 30nm leeward passage; Dragon Bay, Grand Roy, St. George's all visible

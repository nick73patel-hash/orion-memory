# Project 13 — Student Rentals (LCCC Cheyenne, WY)

## What this is
Finding and leasing rental homes near Laramie County Community College (LCCC) in Cheyenne, Wyoming, and renting rooms to college students. Lower cost-of-entry market with potentially strong yields.

## Status
🟡 Starting — concept defined, no work started yet

## Partners
- Solo (user-led)

## Campus details
- **Laramie County Community College (LCCC)**
- 1400 E College Dr, Cheyenne, WY 82007
- Enrollment: ~4,500+ students
- Community college = diverse student age range, many local/working students

## Why Cheyenne/LCCC
- **Lower price point** — Cheyenne home prices significantly below Denver
- **Less competition** — fewer institutional investors in this market
- **Strong yield potential** — lower acquisition cost, solid rental demand near campus
- **Rental arbitrage option** — rent a home, sublease rooms to students (lower capital needed to start)
- Wyoming has no state income tax — favorable for landlords

## Target strategy
- **Start with rental arbitrage** — rent a house, sublease rooms to students (lower risk, lower capital)
- **Validate demand and margins** before acquiring property
- **Graduate to ownership** once model is proven
- **Rent by the room** — 3-4 bedroom houses near campus
- **Unfurnished or lightly furnished** — LCCC has more local students than DU, less need for full furnishings

## Key questions to answer
- What are market rents per bedroom near LCCC?
- Are there enough students seeking off-campus housing? (many LCCC students commute)
- What does a typical 3-4 bed rental cost in that area of Cheyenne?
- Will landlords agree to sublease arrangements?
- What's the realistic occupancy rate given LCCC's commuter student profile?
- Any Wyoming-specific landlord/tenant laws to know?

# Project 14 — Taste of Denmark (Bulk Bakery & Distribution)

## What this is
A wholesale bakery operation built around Ronnie's proven Danish bakery expertise. The model: manufacture the 10 best-selling products in bulk at a single production facility, then distribute them to 30-50 retail locations (cafes, coffee shops, restaurants, delis, hotels, offices) throughout the Denver metro area. Zero retail overhead — pure production and distribution.

## Status
🟡 Starting — concept defined, no work started yet

## Partners
- **Ronnie** — brings the expertise: proven track record running a successful bakery in Denver, knows the products, knows the craft, knows the Denver food scene

## The business model
- **Production**: One central commercial kitchen / production facility
- **Product line**: Top 10 Ronnie-approved Danish bakery products (the winners, not the full menu)
- **Distribution**: Deliver to 30-50 locations throughout Denver metro
- **Revenue target**: Each location sells ~$1,000/day of product
- **At 30 locations**: ~$30,000/day gross revenue = ~$900,000/month
- **At 50 locations**: ~$50,000/day gross revenue = ~$1,500,000/month
- **No retail storefront** = no foot traffic dependency, no retail staff, no retail lease premium

## Why this model works
- Ronnie's products are already proven — customers love them
- Bulk production = dramatically lower cost per unit vs. retail bakery
- 30-50 distribution points = diversified revenue (losing one location = minor impact)
- Coffee shops, cafes, and delis need pastry product but can't bake at scale — this solves their problem
- Danish pastry has a premium positioning — higher margins than commodity baked goods
- Denver's cafe and food scene is thriving and growing
